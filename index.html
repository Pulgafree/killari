About Killari

Killari is the title that gives identity to this fantastic piece of contemporary art. Created and designed by the Argentina Artist, Niza Priot.

Made entirely of wood, the painting has its own character of remarkable warmth. Strong colorful, deep expressions, and a delicate feminine look on his face, allowing his audience closer to deep contemplation.

With serene sweetness, beauty and aesthetic perfection, captivates all eyes. This painting has the ability to attract to himself the admiration of modern society. While the text reads pure and sincere desire to converse again with a loved one.

Its design pays homage faithfully to the characteristic modern Pop Art. Where significance is represented by human beings, with their beliefs and thoughts. A clear tribute to the human mind. The Art has the ability to merge into the history, those who purchase these masterpieces.

The painting was subject to an assessment of the artistic value, carried out within the valuations department conducted by experts from New York in strict privacy. Since its creation, the painting has been concealed and reserved for preserve its modern architecture and prestige. Currently Killari is a closely guarded piece in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

"Killari" is a inimitable jewel of modern contemporary art, which promises to be part within the art history of all time.

About the author

Niza Priot is an Argentine Pop Art painter, graphic artist and sculptor,
recognized above all for her large-scale works of comic art.

Currently working and developing under a blanket of privacy, the works are
made from the beginning with the most exquisite meticulocity, simple and
nuanced with bright colors that invite a vivid psychic and visual stimulation.

Say without words, transport without moving, enter a metaphysical state that
leads to the first causes of art.
